# 05 — The Capture System

> **Your brain is for thinking, not remembering.**

This is one of the most important parts of GIOK. If capture fails, GIOK fails — because an
unrecorded thought is a broken promise to the user. The measure of the capture system is simple:
**can Jake get anything out of his head and into GIOK in under three seconds, from anywhere,
without making a single decision about where it goes?**

## Capture philosophy

1. **Everything is capturable.** A task, a worry, a client detail, a shopping item, an idea at
   the gym, a photo of a whiteboard — all of it belongs in GIOK, instantly.
2. **Zero friction beats perfect structure.** The capture step asks nothing of Jake except the
   thought itself. No mandatory tags, no folder choice, no form. Friction is the enemy.
3. **Capture before organize.** Structure happens *after* the thought is safe. This is Core
   Principle #5 and it is absolute.
4. **Nothing is lost.** Once captured, an item is guaranteed to be seen again — it lands in an
   inbox and is surfaced until it's routed or resolved. Capture is a promise, not a black hole.
5. **Tony organizes, not Jake.** Over time, Tony reads each capture and proposes where it
   belongs. Jake confirms or corrects; Tony learns. The human's job is to *think*; the system's
   job is to *file*.

## Capture channels (how thoughts get in)

Available and future — the system is designed so new channels plug into the same inbox:

- **Text** — type it (available first; the "Ask Tony" / Quick Capture box is the seed of this).
- **Voice** — speak it; transcribed and captured hands-free.
- **Email** — forward or BCC to a GIOK address; the email becomes a capture.
- **Picture** — snap a photo (receipt, whiteboard, business card); image + extracted text.
- **Clipboard** — send whatever's copied straight to the inbox.
- **Android Share** — share from any app into GIOK.
- **Future Watch** — capture from the wrist, voice-first.
- **Future Widget** — a home-screen widget for one-tap capture.

Every channel writes to **one capture inbox** (single source of truth). Channels are just doors
into the same room.

## Routing (what a capture becomes)

Every captured item eventually becomes exactly one of these types, landing in the right
workspace:

| Type | Lands in | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Task** | Action Items / a workspace | "Call the Millers back" |
| **Goal** | Goals | "Get to 100 active clients this year" |
| **Reminder** | the relevant workspace + time | "Checkup for Sarah in March" |
| **Idea** | Learning / Agency / a workspace | "Booth idea for the fair" |
| **Journal** | Family / personal log | "Great morning with the kids" |
| **Shopping** | Home Projects | "Buy furnace filter" |
| **Home** | Home Projects | "Fix the gate latch" |
| **Business** | Agency | "Follow up on the group quote" |
| **Family** | Family | "Anniversary dinner reservation" |
| **Learning** | Learning | "Read that underwriting article" |
| **Health** | Health | "Book the annual physical" |
| **Financial** | Financial | "Review the Q3 budget" |

## How routing works

1. **Capture** → item lands in the inbox with a timestamp and its channel, untyped.
2. **Suggest** → Tony reads it and proposes a type + destination + (optionally) a due date, with
   a short reason.
3. **Confirm/correct** → Jake accepts with one tap or fixes it. Corrections train Tony.
4. **Auto-route (earned)** → for high-confidence, low-risk categories (shopping, home, ideas),
   Tony eventually files automatically and just tells Jake. Anything ambiguous or consequential
   stays confirm-first (People First + AI Assists Humans).

## Design rules for capture
- The capture control is **always reachable** (global shortcut, widget, share target) — never
  more than one action away.
- Capture **never blocks** on network or AI: the item is saved locally first, enriched after.
- The inbox is **honest**: unrouted items are visibly waiting, never silently dropped.
- Routing is **reversible**: any item can be re-typed or moved; nothing is trapped.
- Capture writes to the single source of truth and is **never duplicated** across channels.

The capture system is where "your brain is for thinking, not remembering" stops being a slogan
and becomes a guarantee.
