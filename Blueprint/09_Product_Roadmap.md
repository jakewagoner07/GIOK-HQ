# 09 — Product Roadmap

This is the *product* roadmap — the arc of what GIOK becomes. It is intentionally higher-level
than the technical build log in `tony-alpha/ROADMAP.md`; that file records how the desktop app
was built, this file records where the product is going. When they discuss the same thing, this
document sets the intent and the technical roadmap serves it.

Guiding rule: **each version must stand on its own as genuinely useful.** No version is a
scaffold that only pays off later — every release must save Jake time today (Core Principle #9).

---

## Version 1 — The Command Center (now)
*Goal: GIOK becomes the single, trustworthy place Jake runs his day from.*

- Executive **Home** dashboard answering "what should Jake focus on today?"
- **Registry** as the single source of truth; **AI Workforce** (agents) tracked and monitored.
- **Interactive Action Items** and the first **Capture** surface ("Ask Tony" / Quick Capture).
- **Mission Control** (in-app + second-monitor window).
- **Dark executive branding**, theme system, multi-window support.
- **Tony** as command router and recommender (local, no external AI yet).

*Definition of done:* Jake opens GIOK every morning by choice because it makes his day clearer.

## Version 2 — The Chief of Staff
*Goal: Tony becomes genuinely proactive; capture becomes effortless and everywhere.*

- **Full Capture System** — voice, email, photo, clipboard, Android share → one inbox.
- **Tony auto-routing** captures to type + workspace, learning from corrections.
- **Live integrations begin:** Calendar and Email feed the Morning Brief and priorities; GHL
  feeds the Agency and Checkup pipeline.
- **Life Score & Business Score** live, with trends and weekly coaching from Tony.
- **The full workspace set** (Goals, Non-Negotiables, Health, Financial, Family, Home, Learning).
- **The annual Giok Checkup** runs as a first-class, tracked ritual.

*Definition of done:* Tony tells Jake what matters before Jake asks, and files what Jake dumps.

## Version 3 — Anywhere & Proactive
*Goal: GIOK is with Jake everywhere and acts on earned autonomy.*

- **Mobile app** — capture, notifications, Mission Control, widgets, voice (see
  [11_Mobile_Vision.md](11_Mobile_Vision.md)).
- **Cloud sync** across desktop, mobile, and web.
- **Earned automation:** Tony drafts outreach, prepares Checkups, and auto-handles low-risk,
  reversible work — always inside granted, revocable permissions.
- **Deeper integrations:** finances, wearables, e-sign/quoting.
- **Proactive coaching** against goals and scores, with early-warning nudges.

*Definition of done:* Jake trusts GIOK to run the machinery so he can focus on people and vision.

## Long-term vision
*Goal: GIOK becomes a category — the operating system for people who take ownership of their life.*

- **A workforce of AI agents** doing real work across the business, coordinated and monitored.
- **Multi-user:** other disciplined operators — agents, small-business owners, "Founders" — run
  their lives on GIOK; the branding/theme system already anticipates this.
- **Plugin system, API, and marketplace** so the product extends without breaking its core (see
  [12_Future_Architecture.md](12_Future_Architecture.md)).
- **Ambient presence:** wearables, vehicle, smart home — capture and awareness wherever life
  happens.
- **The endgame:** GIOK is how serious people run a disciplined life and business — calm,
  honest, and always answering "what should I focus on today?"

---

**Sequencing principle:** capture and truth come before automation; awareness comes before
action; trust is earned before autonomy is granted. GIOK grows in that order, deliberately.
