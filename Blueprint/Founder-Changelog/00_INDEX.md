# The GIOK Founder Changelog

*The story of GIOK as it is being built.*

This is not technical documentation and not a specification. It's a **founder's journal** — the
record of *why* GIOK is being built the way it is. Each entry captures the problem that was in
front of us, the decision we made, the reasoning behind it, what we learned, and the future ideas
it unlocked.

The specifications live in the rest of the [Blueprint](../00_README.md). This changelog **points
to** those documents; it never repeats them. Read the Blueprint to know *what* GIOK is. Read this
to know *why* it became that.

Every entry from Project Diamond forward is held to **[Project Diamond](../13_Project_Diamond.md)** —
the standard that GIOK stopped chasing features and began perfecting experiences. Quality over
quantity, always.

> **People Matter More Than Money.**
> **Your brain is for thinking, not remembering.**
> **GIOK helps you become better, not busy.**

## How entries are written
Every entry follows the same shape: **Date · Version · Problem · Decision · Reasoning · Lessons
Learned · Future Ideas · Related Blueprint Documents.** Written in the moment, honest about the
messy parts, and kept forever.
